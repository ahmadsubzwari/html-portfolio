How are these utensils made?

  1. During sugarcane harvest season, a byproduct known as baggasse is created in bulk quantities
  2. We collect this baggasse (which is usually gone to waste) and bring it into our factories for manufacturing
  3. We create a great alternative to plastic and wood by using baggasse utensils instead of wooden/plastic utensils
  4. by purchasing and using baggasse utensils, you would essentially be:
    • decreasing rate of climate change(less plastics burnt)
    • decreasing abundance of landfill sites(baggasse biodegrades quickly)
    • decreasing net deforestation(less wood cut down)
    • etc
